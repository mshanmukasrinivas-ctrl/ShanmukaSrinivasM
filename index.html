About Me

I am a doctoral research scholar in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at IIT Tirupati. My research focuses on viscoelastic abrasive finishing, additive manufacturing post-processing, ultra-precision finishing, surface metrology, tribology, and rheology.

My doctoral work deals with the development and application of viscoelastic abrasive finishing tools for improving the surface quality of additively manufactured surgical stainless steel.
