Research Experience

Doctoral Research Work

IIT Tirupati

Developed viscoelastic abrasive finishing tools for surface improvement of additively manufactured surgical stainless steel. The work involved tool fabrication, process experimentation, surface characterization, tribology, rheology, and analytical modelling.

Major Research Skills

  • Viscoelastic abrasive finishing
  • Additive manufacturing post-processing
  • Surface roughness and areal surface texture measurement
  • Tool wear analysis
  • Tribology testing
  • Rheological characterization
  • SEM-based surface morphology analysis
  • Analytical modelling of finishing processes

Materials Handled

  • Additively manufactured SS316L stainless steel
  • Surgical stainless steel
  • Inconel 718
  • Polymer-based abrasive finishing tools
